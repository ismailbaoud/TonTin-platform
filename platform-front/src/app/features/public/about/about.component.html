Our Mission

We believe in the power of community-driven finance. TonTin transforms the age-old tradition of rotating savings and credit associations (ROSCAs) into a modern, blockchain-based platform that empowers individuals and communities worldwide.

By leveraging smart contracts and cryptocurrency, we eliminate the need for intermediaries, reduce costs, and ensure complete transparency in every transaction.

What is a Dâr?

A Dâr (from Arabic "دار" meaning "house" or "circle") is a rotating savings circle where members contribute regularly and take turns receiving the collective pot.
